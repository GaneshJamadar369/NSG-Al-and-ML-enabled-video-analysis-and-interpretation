About NSG

The National Security Guard (NSG) — India's elite counter-terrorism and crisis response unit, powered by technology and precision.

Established in 1984, NSG operates as a specialized force for counter-terrorism, hostage rescue, and VIP security. With AI-enabled data analytics, surveillance integration, and anomaly detection modules, NSG Supervisors can now make faster, smarter, and mission-critical decisions.

Mission

To protect national integrity through rapid, data-driven, and intelligent response systems — powered by AI and human excellence.
